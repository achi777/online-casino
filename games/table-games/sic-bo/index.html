How to Play Sic Bo

  • Small: Total 4-10 (excluding triples). Pays 1:1
  • Big: Total 11-17 (excluding triples). Pays 1:1
  • Odd/Even: Odd or even total (excluding triples). Pays 1:1
  • Specific Total: Bet on exact sum (4-17). Higher payouts for rarer totals
  • Single Number: Bet on any number 1-6. Pays 1:1, 2:1, or 3:1 based on appearances
  • Any Triple: Any three of a kind (111, 222, etc.). Pays 30:1
  • Specific Triple: Choose specific triple. Pays 180:1
